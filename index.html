2. How do you ensure the websites you build are mobile-friendly?

I use responsive design principles with HTML, CSS, and Bootstrap to ensure that all websites are optimized for various devices and screen sizes, providing a seamless user experience on both mobile and desktop platforms.

3. What is your process for starting a new project?

My process involves understanding your requirements, planning the project scope, designing wireframes, and then developing the site using HTML, CSS, jQuery, and Bootstrap. I maintain close communication throughout to ensure your vision is realized.
